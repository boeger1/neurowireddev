Who Am i

Hallo world and welcome to my site My name is Matias, I am twenty-nine years old and live in Denmark, a lovely country with a few days of sunny weather. I have been living here all my life and moved around quite a bit. for now, I am located in Roskilde with my lovely girlfriend.

You might think, "Yeah, that’s all fine, but that doesn't tell me much about you," and you're right. Well, let's see… I love to build things. I have always been creative, but not in the "I like to paint things" way—rather, I love building things myself.I am used to making things out of wood, and I think that’s where my interest in programming comes from It’s just building things in another way.

One of my big hobbies is gaming, where MMORPGs are my go-to. I love the big fantasy worlds, and I am amazed by other people's imagination and how they are able to create such magnificent worlds. I hope to be a part of that one day.
